Zola'u Nìprrte' | Welcome

The point of this website is to help and teach anyone and everyone who is willing to learn the Na'vi language. If you are discouraged and intimidated by advanced linguist terms, this is the place for you. I try to be as simple as possible here in all my explanations of the grammar.
